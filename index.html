Dark Glass Window Tinting

About us

Our window tinting kits come in 5 different levels of darkness to suit every requirement. The higher the number the more light is let through. 5% is a very dark tint and 70% is a very light tint

Benefits

The application of window tinting film to your vehicle offers many advantages. Here are just a few reasons to install window tint:
  • Reduces Glare
  • Offers an unique finish
  • Creates a cool and comfortable environment
  • Protects against UV rays
  • Adds shatter resistance
